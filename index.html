How It Works

Three steps to a smarter consultation

From first symptom to clinical summary — automatically, continuously, without extra work from your team.

01

Patient describes symptoms

The patient opens Catalynk and is coached by an AI that understands natural language. It guides them through describing their symptoms clearly — asking targeted follow-up questions, drawing out details patients often forget to mention, and building a complete clinical picture, not just a checkbox list.

02

Symptoms evolve in real time

As the patient waits, they log how their condition changes. Catalynk builds a timestamped clinical thread — capturing severity trends, new developments, and progression that a single intake form can never show.

03

Doctor receives the full picture

By the time the appointment starts, your doctor has an AI-generated clinical summary — what happened, when, how it progressed, and likely differentials. No wasted time. Every minute counts.

Features

Built for the
clinical workflow

Everything your team needs to run smarter consultations — without changing how you work.

Conversational AI Triage

Patients are actively coached through describing their symptoms — our AI asks the right follow-up questions, prompts for details they'd otherwise miss, and adapts to their health history to build a complete clinical picture. No forms, no guesswork.

Dynamic Urgency Scoring

Every patient is assigned a real-time urgency score that updates as their symptoms evolve. Your reception team sees who needs to be seen first — backed by AI, not gut feel.

Thread-Based Symptom Tracking

Patients log symptom changes as they happen. A timestamped thread shows progression over days — giving your doctor temporal context, not just today's snapshot.

Auto-Generated Clinical Summaries

Before each appointment, Catalynk produces a structured summary: key symptoms, timeline, severity trend, and AI-suggested differentials — ready before the doctor enters the room.

Prioritised Clinic Dashboard

Your team sees every patient's status at a glance — urgency level, chief complaint, duration, and prep materials. Manage your day with clarity, not chaos.

Privacy & Compliance First

Built for GDPR and NHS data standards from day one. All patient data encrypted at rest and in transit. On-premise deployment options available for strict data sovereignty requirements.
